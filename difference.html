José Alvarez

What difference does it make

June 2024

What difference does it make

If I wake up at 3:45 am

If I do exercise early in the morning

If I shower

If I go to mass

If I have breakfast

If I do work

If I do it diligently

If I choose to stop at around 5 pm

If I have dinner and get some rest

If I study some math

If I do whatever else

And then go to sleep

If I pray

If I humble myself and live my daily life with honesty, and that's it

Or if I inspire myself and strive to do extraordinary things

"Persevere in the exact fulfillment of the obligations of the moment. That work—humble, monotonous, small—is prayer expressed in action that prepares you to receive the grace of the other work—great and wide and deep—of which you dream." — Saint Josemaría Escrivá

And what are those obligations?

To concentrate in my software engineering work

To do my best to push as many commits and PRs as possible

And hopefully not get stuck

What difference does it make, if I apologize with those I've wronged

What difference does it make, if I just let go and move on

What difference does it make, if I reach out to those I miss, who may not miss me back

What difference does it make, if I leave Guatemala

What difference does it make, if I stay in Guatemala

What difference does it make, if I go to the US

What difference does it make, if I go somewhere else

What difference does it make, if I focus on politics

What difference does it make, if I focus on science and engineering

What difference does it make, if I choose to be outspoken about my thoughts

What difference does it make, if I talk and talk and talk about what I believe it's wrong in the world

The world will keep spinning anyways, and the sun will come out tomorrow

I am not perfect anyways, no one is

We'll die anyways

God will carry out His plans

It would be arrogant to believe we have that much control, yet it probably feels wrong to be indifferent

What difference does it make, to just live my life outside the noise

What difference does it make, to engage in the noise and be loud

We are not as irrelevant as we think we are when we are sad, and probably not as important as we wish we were when we are arrogant

Why do humans yearn for being important

The coolest people are down to earth, humble and authentic

What difference does it make, if I post this, or keep it to myself
